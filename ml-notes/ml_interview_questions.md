# Machine Learning Interview Questions

## Linear Regression

Q: When should Linear Regression be used?

A: When the target variable is continuous, such as house prices or sales prediction.

---

## Logistic Regression

Q: Why is Logistic Regression used for classification?

A: Because it predicts probabilities between 0 and 1 using the sigmoid function.

---

## Decision Trees

Q: What is overfitting in Decision Trees?

A: When the tree memorizes training data and performs poorly on new data.

---

## KNN

Q: What does K represent?

A: The number of nearest neighbors considered for prediction.

---

## Random Forest

Q: Why is Random Forest better than a single Decision Tree?

A: It combines multiple trees, reducing overfitting and improving accuracy.

---

## SVM

Q: What is a hyperplane?

A: The decision boundary that separates classes.

---

## Naive Bayes

Q: What is the main assumption?

A: Features are independent of each other.
